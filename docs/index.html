Hello,  I'm 

AmzroSevca.
Pronounciation:
"AM-zroh-SEV-ca"
I am an ambitious interdisciplinary designer with a Bachelor of Fine Arts who is highly intrigued by mixing together various artistic mediums to produce memorable experiences. My work involves intertwining technology and design with projects that feature graphic design, software development, photography, audio engineering, web design, illustration, video art, and other practices.

Often you can find me researching design aesthetics, composing music, hosting live streams, or putting together a brand kit for a client on most days. This website that you are viewing was built with Asveora, one of the biggest projects that I have created to date. It is a digital network, toolbox, and resource library that aims to help individuals quickly build their own homes on the internet where they can manage their own content.




Work History
I attended Bowling Green State University from 2020 to 2024 in pursuit of a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree with a specialization in Graphic Design. During my tenure there I also studied video production, audio engineering, various forms of software development, digital illustration, web design, mixed media art, and installation art.

Much of my professional experience has been with small scale organizations and freelance projects concerned with environmental sustainability, media abstractions, community welfare, experiential simulations, and aesthetic studies. In addition I have also worked extensively in the realm of sales and marketing where I have operated as a direct sales agent and retail sales advisor for companies such as Best Buy, Hoffman Weber Construction, T.J. Maxx, Macy’s, etc.

AMZROSEVCA STUDIOS · 2020
Asveora Network Engine
Digital network project built with the goal of bringing the internet back to a more human place while addressing issues that have come with the hyper centralized internet of the 2010s such as a lack of organic exploration, unnatural conversations, deluges of advertisements, little control over user data, etc.
BGSU SCHOOL OF ART · 2022
Seagate Packaging
Crafted alternative packaging for Seagate's hard drives as a part of a rebranding project that saw me selecting the product of a company and reimagining its presentation. The goal was a visually exciting and fun aesthetic that would make the box more than just functional material.
AMZROSEVCA STUDIOS · 2020
Xaosphora Worlds
Xaosphora is a rewriting project that rearranges the contents of fictional universes for fun. It was launched in 2020 focusing on bringing new ideas to the table using the preexisting content of various movie, video game, and novel series in the hopes of expanding upon them or offering alternate takes on the worlds.
AMZROSEVCA STUDIOS · 2023
Rhoxena Point & Click
Rhoxena is an interactive point-and-click web project using only HTML, CSS, and JS. The project is inspired by various sci-fi works such as Marathon, Dead Space, Metroid, System Shock, and Halo. The story follows an unidentified agent who is sent to a research colony on a volatile planet to investigate a mysterious distress signal.
BGSU SCHOOL OF ART · 2022
Community Oversight
Following protests against police misconduct that occurred at the beginning of the 2020s, I created an infographic explaining why initiatives such as community oversight can be valuable to ensuring that policing is done properly to the benefit of the community's inhabitants.
BGSU SCHOOL OF ART · 2023
Death of the Store
Death of the Store is a short magazine discussing the challenges that brick-and-mortar stores have been facing since the change in the shopping landscape with fierce competition from online shopping. It analyzes some of the trends and primary factors that have lead to many physical locations packing up and leaving the market.
